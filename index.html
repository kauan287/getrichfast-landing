About the book

This book is for every young person who lives passionately about the idea of being free and alive. If you ever had the thought: I want, I need, I will be rich and then I'll be free — this book will help you in a big way.

You might wonder about the other part of the title, or/and die Young. Talking to many people, all of them related to the first part of the title at some point in their lives, but as they grew older, they shifted their priorities and forgot their own intentions. Now they believe they'll realistically never be rich. The thought of becoming wealthy died young in them.

According to the National Study of Millionaires, over 70% of millionaires made their first million between the ages of 30 and 59 — right when most people give up on their dreams and settle for a "realistic" life. People like that "die" young: they killed the adult they once dreamed of becoming.

Only 7% make their first million before 35. We live in a time where young people have already achieved generational wealth, and that shouldn't stop us — it should motivate us. Here we'll also see how envy is just a feeling for the rich but for the poor it's a prison. Will you kill the adult you once dreamed of becoming or will you live to tell the story?
